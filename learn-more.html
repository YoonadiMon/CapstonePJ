Why Recycle E-Waste?

🌍

Protect the Environment

E-waste contains toxic materials like lead and mercury that can leak into soil and water. Proper recycling prevents pollution and protects ecosystems.

💰

Recover Valuable Resources

Electronics contain gold, silver, copper, and rare earth elements. Recycling recovers these materials, reducing the need for mining.

👥

Community Health

Safe disposal protects communities from hazardous exposure and creates green jobs in the recycling industry.
