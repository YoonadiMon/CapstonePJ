About Us

Discover how we're transforming electronic waste recycling for a sustainable future.

Discover Our Story

At AfterVolt, we are dedicated to transforming how electronic waste is managed in our communities. Built by a team of ICT students from Asia Pacific University, our platform represents a fresh approach to e-waste recycling—one that prioritizes accessibility, transparency, and user engagement. We saw a critical gap: while policies and licensed recyclers exist, public participation remains low due to inconvenient processes and lack of information.

Our commitment goes beyond just building a system. We believe that technology can bridge the gap between people who want to recycle responsibly and the infrastructure that makes it possible. By creating a centralized digital platform, we simplify the entire journey—from scheduling pickups to tracking requests and accessing proper disposal guidelines. Every feature is designed with one goal: making it easier for everyone to do their part.

We're not just developers; we're advocates for environmental awareness. Our vision embraces the circular economy, where discarded electronics are seen not as waste, but as valuable resources waiting to be recovered. Join us in this journey. Together, we can turn e-waste into opportunity and build a more sustainable future for Malaysia.

Our Vision

We strive to create a cleaner and healthier planet by responsibly managing electronic waste, preventing it from ending up in landfills, and promoting a circular economy for electronics.

Our Mission

To be your one-stop solution for electronic waste recycling—connecting you to the right collectors, providing clear disposal guidance, and making responsible recycling simple and rewarding for everyone.
